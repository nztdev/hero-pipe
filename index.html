The pipeline

From idea to live hero
in four steps

No design experience needed. Describe what you're building and HeroPipe handles the rest — scroll chapters, camera paths, particle systems, and all.

01

Describe your project

Tell HeroPipe what you're building. A sentence is enough. "A community platform for indie game developers" is all it needs.

02

Engine configures the scene

The rule engine maps your input to a theme, camera path, particle system, and color palette — instantly, locally, no API required.

03

Optional AI refinement

Plug in your Gemini API key and the model acts as a creative director — context-aware, capability-bound, and free on the generous free tier.

04

Export and deploy

Download a single self-contained HTML file. Drop it on GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel — anywhere. Zero dependencies, zero lock-in.

What you get

Everything a modern hero
section should have

Built on Three.js and GSAP. Runs entirely in the browser. Performs on mobile. Looks expensive.

⬡

Scroll-driven chapters

Five keyframed scroll stages that guide visitors through your story without them realising they're being guided.

✦

Particle systems

Aura, network, nebula, constellation — each preset is tuned to communicate a specific feeling, not just look pretty.

◎

Camera choreography

Ascend, orbit, drift, push — cinematic camera paths that make a scroll feel like a scene.

◈

Theme presets

Anime, AI, community, nature, fintech, and more. Each is a complete visual language — palette, motion, and type — not just a colour swap.

⟐

Performance tiers

Automatically detects device capability and adjusts quality. Full WebGL on desktop, graceful fallback on mobile.

◇

AI creative direction

Gemini reads the engine's full capability manifest before responding. It can only suggest things that actually exist.
